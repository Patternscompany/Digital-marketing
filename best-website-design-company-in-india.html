FAQs

Facebook ads are a form of online advertising that utilises the Facebook platform to promote products, services, or brands to a big audience. They are an important part of digital marketing strategies, used by businesses of all sizes due to their wide reach and the platform’s sophisticated targeting capabilities.

Facebook offers a variety of ad types to cater to different marketing goals and strategies, making it a versatile platform for advertisers. Understanding these different types can help in creating more effective Facebook ad campaigns.

Measuring the results of your Facebook ad campaign is important to understand its effectiveness and to make data-driven decisions for future advertising strategies. Facebook provides several tools and metrics within its Ads Manager that help in assessing your campaign’s performance.

Facebook ads are suitable for a wide range of businesses, from small local shops to large multinational corporations. This versatility is due to Facebook’s huge user base and its sophisticated targeting options, which allow businesses of all types and sizes to reach their specific audience effectively.

Facebook Ads are known for their powerful targeting capabilities. They allow you to target users based on demographics, interests, behaviours, and more. This makes Facebook Ads ideal for building brand awareness and reaching a specific audience with precision. Facebook’s visual format is also great for engaging ad campaigns that are visually appealing and storytelling in nature.

Yes, having a Facebook page is a requirement to run Facebook ads. A Facebook page serves as the anchor for your advertising on the platform. It’s where your ads are associated with and often the first point of contact for users interacting with your ads. The page represents your business’s presence on Facebook and provides a space where you can engage with your audience, share content, and present your brand through Facebook ads.
